Rivian R1T Accessories Guide

Truck-focused picks for the R1T — tonneau covers, bed racks, floor mats, and utility upgrades. For the full R1T deep-dive, visit R1TParts.com.

1. Tesmanian Retractable Tonneau Cover

Hard retractable tonneau cover designed specifically for the R1T bed. Aluminum slats with weather seals keep cargo dry in rain and snow. Locks at any position along the rail for flexible bed access. One of the most popular R1T upgrades.

Pros

  • Hard retractable aluminum construction
  • Weather-sealed for rain and snow
  • Locks at any position
  • Clean low-profile appearance
  • Designed for R1T specifically

Cons

  • Significant investment ($800+)
  • Adds weight to the bed
  • Can't haul tall items when closed

2. YAKIMA OverHaul HD Bed Rack

Heavy-duty bed rack for the R1T that mounts to the stake pockets. Supports rooftop tents, kayaks, lumber, bikes — up to 500 lbs of gear. Adjustable height positions. Works with or without a tonneau cover depending on configuration.

Pros

  • 500 lb load capacity
  • Adjustable height positions
  • Stake pocket mounting (no drilling)
  • Compatible with YAKIMA accessories

Cons

  • Premium price ($500+)
  • Heavy when installed
  • May conflict with some tonneau covers
